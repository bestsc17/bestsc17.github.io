BEST Chisinau Summer Course 2017

All The Shades Of Cabernet

About

How much do you know about Moldova?

No matter how deep is your knowledge in geography, you definitely know that Moldova is famous for its 5000 year history in winemaking—the art of producing one of the most aristocratic drinks—wine.


Do you want to be a little bit more aristocratic?

This summer, 22 students will have a chance to learn about winemaking in the BEST way, by learning history, composition and the process of creating this beautiful drink.

During 9 days of the course, you will be introduced into the topic by theoretical lectures and practical workshops in laboratories, facilitated by professors and specialists in this field. In addition, participants will have the possibility to visit one of the top winemaking companies from Moldova.

Still not motivated to apply?

Take the chance to discover a new country by exploring its traditions, places and people and live this 9 days the moldavian way!

Visit our unique capital - Chisinau and meet new people from all over the Europe to create unforgettable memories together.

Take part in a wild weekend trip and visit mysterious underground wine cellars.

Apply to the BEST Course “All The Shades Of Cabernet” and have one of the most awesome journeys this summer!
